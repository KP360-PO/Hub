SOP: ACCOUNTING GC CONCERN

 

CONFIRMING WHETHER RECEIVED OR NOT

1. Upon reviewing the message, acknowledge receipt by clicking the 👍 (Like) reaction to confirm that the concern has been noted.

2. Identify the Purchase Order (PO) number indicated in the concern (e.g., PO: CL260115D-NJ-2). Log in to NetSuite (NS), then navigate to Transactions > Purchases > Enter Purchase Orders > List. Enter the PO number in the PO Number field

3. Open the Purchase Order by right-clicking View and selecting Open in new tab. Once the PO is open, use Ctrl + F to search for the concerned SKU (e.g., 331-1933L-US). Review the item line details and verify the status. If the notes indicate OOS in EOD informed AAG, this confirms the item is out of stock and that AAG has already been notified.

 

 

4. To verify whether the item was received, go to the Related Records tab within the Purchase Order. Under Receipts & Bills, locate the Item Receipt entry, then right-click the Date and select Open link in new tab to view the receipt details and confirm receipt status.

5. Once the Item Receipt opens in a new tab, go to the Communication tab and select the Files subtab. Review the attached Pick List document, then click the file to open it and confirm receipt details related to the concerned item.

6. After opening the Pick List file, review the document to locate the concerned SKU and check for any notes from the warehouse team. Warehouse notes are typically added when an item is not received. If the Pick List indicates a note such as Not Received for the concerned SKU, this confirms that the item was not received.

7. Take a screenshot of the Pick List clearly showing the concerned SKU and the note indicating Not Received. Return to the Accounting w/ PO Team group chat, reply directly to the original message, attach the screenshot as supporting evidence, confirm that the item was not received, and click Send to submit the response.

 

CLOSING THE PO IF THERE IS NO OTHER INVOICE

For concerns requesting PO closure due to no other expected invoices, acknowledge the message by clicking the 👍 (Like) reaction. Identify the concerned PO number from the message, then verify the PO in NetSuite by following Step 2.

Check the PO line items to confirm that the Quantity Ordered matches the Quantity Received and that there are no pending or expected invoices. If all items are fully received even if not billed and no further invoices are expected, proceed with closing the Purchase Order in NetSuite.

Once validation is complete and no further invoices are expected, click the Close button on the Purchase Order. Confirm that the PO status changes from Pending Bill to Closed to ensure the Purchase Order has been successfully closed.

Reply Done to the group chat message to confirm that the concern has been addressed and the Purchase Order action has been closed.

 

 

KSI SKU PICK UP TOMORROW / OOS IN EOD

For concerns involving KSI SKUs with remarks such as OOS in EOD or Pickup Tomorrow, acknowledge the message by clicking the 👍 (Like) reaction. Identify the concerned PO number and repeat Steps 2 and 3 to review the Purchase Order in NetSuite, check the SKU status, and validate the remarks before confirming whether the item is received or for credit memo.

If the KSI SKU shows OOS in EOD and is marked as closed in the Purchase Order, verify this by checking the next day s Pick List. Open the Pick List for the following PO date (e.g., CL251226K-NJ), noting that the warehouse was closed on 12/25, which is why the next available Pick List date is 12/26. Review the Pick List to confirm that the SKU does not appear, validating that the item was closed due to out-of-stock status.

Go to the Pick List SharePoint and navigate to New Jersey > Will Call > done since the concern is for NJ. Search for the 12/26 batch and open the corresponding Pick List file to verify whether the concerned SKU appears, confirming its status Picklist SharePoint: https://netorgft5128391.sharepoint.com/sites/Warehouse/Shared%20Documents/Forms/AllItems.aspx?id=%2Fsites%2FWarehouse%2FShared%20Documents%2FWarehouse%20Pick%20Sheets&p=true&ga=1

Download and open the Pick List file for the corresponding batch and review the document details. Locate the concerned SKU and verify the order date and Customer PO. If the Pick List confirms that the item was received on 12/26, this validates that the SKU was received despite being previously marked as OOS in EOD.

Go back to NetSuite, open the Purchase Order, and click Edit. Locate the concerned SKU line, uncheck the box that marked it as closed or OOS, then click OK to save the changes.

After saving the changes, confirm that the PO status updates to Partially Received. Click the Receive button to receive the item that was previously marked as closed and complete the receipt confirmation.

After clicking Receive, an Item Receipt window will open. Enter the batch number in the Memo field and the Reference PO. Set Received By to your name and select Class: Will Call.

Go to the Communication tab, attach the supporting Pick List file, then click Save to complete the Item Receipt process.

 

 

FOR KSOURCE CONCERN

For KSource concerns, acknowledge the message by clicking the 👍 (Like) reaction. Identify the PO batch number listed in the concern, then repeat Step 2 to search and review the Purchase Order in NetSuite.

Open the KSource/Tonsa spreadsheet, then use Ctrl + F to search for the concerned PO batch number. Check the tracking number and shipment status. If there is movement on the tracking, this confirms that the supplier has received and shipped the order.
