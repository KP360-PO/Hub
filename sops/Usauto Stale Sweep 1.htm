SOP: USAuto Stale

 

To check and resolve stale USAuto orders in SS that remain in Awaiting Status for more than 4 hours, ensuring timely PO submission and proper order handling.

 

Process:

1. Identify Stale Orders

Navigate to:

Log in to SS > Navigate to Awaiting Status > Apply filter by Item SKU to align orders with the same SKU > Review and identify all USAuto SKUs > Identify orders older than 4 hours.

 

 

2. Investigate Automation Failure

        Review each stale order

        Investigate why the order was not included in ZM automation

        Confirm that the order did not successfully go through automation

        Order Received (Thank you order email)

o   If a confirmation is received:

o   Manually move the order status to On Hold in SS

o   Add internal notes with Sales Order ID.

 

        Order Not Received ( No Thank You Order)

o   If no confirmation email is received or a No Thank You response is received:

        Process the order manually (Send PO in ZM /Manually Place in PFG if applicable)

 

3. Send PO

1.   Send PO for all USAuto orders older than 4 hours

2.   Wait for a confirmation email from USAuto
