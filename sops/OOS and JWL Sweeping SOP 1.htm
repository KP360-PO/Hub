SOP: OOS Sweeping

 

Review Backordered and OOS orders and determine whether they can still be fulfilled by reordering from a different warehouse or cross-fulfilling from another supplier, as long as the order remains profitable.

 

Process:

1. Identify OOS / Backordered Orders

        Log in to ShipStation

        Apply filter for OOS Tag

        Review all orders for the day

        Prioritize:

o    Ship-By-Day orders

o    Time-sensitive shipments

2. Fulfillment Review by Supplier

A. Will Call Orders

        Go to ZM

        Check if the item can still be fulfilled from:

o    A different warehouse, or

o    An alternate supplier

        Confirm availability and cost

        Proceed only if fulfillment is profitable

 

B. Dropship Orders

        Check availability using:

o    PMB or supplier s website directly

        Confirm stock status and shipping availability

        Validate pricing and profitability before proceeding

 

3. Based on availability and cost:

         Reorder from a different warehouse or supplier, or

         Cross-fulfill with an alternate supplier, or

         If not profitable or confirmed OOS:

o    Proceed with cancellation

 

 

4. Cancellation Process (Confirmed OOS)

A.  Verify SKU Cancellation History in Amazon and Shipstation

        Check the SKU in Amazon and Shipstation to confirm there has been no cancellation within the last 30 days.

 

        If a cancellation exists within 30 days:

o    Verify whether the order was cancelled by the customer (CX). If cancelled by CX, ensure there is no record in SS, ZM, or CA.

o    If the same SKU was cancelled within 30 days for any other reason, do NOT proceed with cancellation. Send JWL instead to avoid violations.

 

B. Confirmed No Cancellation within 30 Days

 

 

Once confirmed that the SKU has no cancellation within the last 30 days, proceed with the cancellation process:

        Email the customer first to inform them of the cancellation.

        Cancel the order in Amazon.

        Cancel the order in SS.

        Cancel the order in CA.

        Update notes in the OOS tracker accordingly, including:

o   Netsuite (Willcall)

o   Spreadsheet (willcall)

o   Zenship (Willcall)

o   Channel Advisor (DropShip)

 

 

SOP: JWL Sweeping

 

This applies to eBay orders (orders are not cancelled in eBay) and for Amazon orders with a cancellation within 30 days.

 

Important:

JWL Cut-Off Time:

        The daily cut-off time for JWL Sweeping is 2:00 PM EST.

        Orders must be included in JWL before 2:00 PM EST so the warehouse can ship the order to the customer.

 

Process:

1. Review Orders with JWL Tags

        Log in to ShipStation

        Apply filter for Exception / JWL

        Review all orders for the day

        Prioritize:

o    Ship-By-Day orders

o    Time-sensitive shipments

 

 

2. Fulfillment Review by Supplier

A. Will Call Orders

        Go to ZM

        Check if the item can still be fulfilled from:

o    A different warehouse, or

o    An alternate supplier

        Confirm availability and cost

        Proceed only if fulfillment is profitable

 

B. Dropship Orders

        Check availability using:

o    PMB or supplier s website directly

        Confirm stock status and shipping availability

        Validate pricing and profitability before proceeding

 

3. Based on availability and cost:

  • Reorder from a different warehouse or supplier, or
  • Cross-fulfill with an alternate supplier, or
  • If not profitable or confirmed OOS:
    • Proceed with sending JWL

 

4. JWL Process (Confirmed OOS)

         Select all Confirmed OOS orders for JWL processing.

         Edit Package Details

o    Update dimensions as follows:

        Weight: 4 lbs

        Dimensions (L x W x H): 10 x 2 x 1

o    Set Ship from Location to KP360 NJ.

 

         Apply JWL Note

o    Add JWL note as an item in Custom Field 1:

Select all orders > Go to Other Actions > Choose Apply Bulk Action > Select Set Customer Field 1 > Enter JWL

         Generate Shipping Labels

o    Select the cheapest available shipping labels.

o    Create / generate the shipping labels.

o    Download the labels and upload them to the Warehouse SharePoint.

 

         Tracker

o    Track all JWL orders in the online JWL tracking spreadsheet.

o    Post the daily JWL list in the OOS JWL Channel.

o    Notify Anthony with the list of JWL orders for the day.

 

         Email & Refund

o    Send JWL email to the customer.

o    Refund the order (see Walmart exceptions below).

 

Important Reminders

Walmart Orders

        Use UPS or FedEx tracking only.

        Do NOT use AMZL tracking for Walmart orders.

        If a Walmart order was shipped via AMZL and requires valid tracking:

JWL may still be sent - DO NOT refund the customer, since the order is still being shipped to them.
