SOP: EOD Tracker Sweeping

 

To review all orders listed in the Warehouse EOD Tracker at the end of day and act on orders that were not received and remain pending in ZS, ensuring timely reorder, cross-fulfillment, or cancellation.

 

Process:

1. Review EOD Tracker

        Access the Warehouse EOD Tracker at end of day

        Review all listed orders, confirm that listed orders were not received by the warehouse.

 

2: Evaluate Fulfillment Options

 

For each pending orders, check item availability determine whether the order can be:

        Reordered

        Cross-fulfilled

        Marked OOS for Cancellation / JWL

 

3. Action Taken

A. Reorder - Reorder the pending part through the appropriate supplier. Update notes in ZS, NS PO, and NS Invoice accordingly

B. Cross-Fulfill - Cross-fulfill the part if available from Dropship Suppliers. Update ZS, NS PO, and NS Invoice

C. OOS Cancellation / JWL - If item is confirmed Out of Stock, mark for Cancellation or JWL. Follow cancellation/JWL procedure.

 

4. Update Warehouse EOD Tracker Spreadsheet
