SOP: All Hold Order Sweeping (Duplicate Orders)

 

Ensure all Hold orders in ZM are reviewed daily to identify and resolve duplicate orders shipping to the same address. This process helps prevent duplicate shipments, incorrect quantities, and unnecessary fulfillment.

 

Process:

1. Log in to ZM

Navigate to:

ZM > POS > Order > ALL> Apply filter to Hold orders only

 

2. Identify Duplicate Orders

        Review the filtered Hold orders list

        Customer Verification (If needed) - reach out to the customer to verify quantity.

        Use discretion based on order review:

        Approve Order(s) if:

o   Items are needed for multiple uses

o   Order is confirmed as a valid replacement

o   Customer intentionally placed multiple orders

o   Ship to Business/Commercial Address

        Decline / Cancel Order(s) if:

o   Orders are confirmed duplicates

o   Quantities are not justified

o   Customer confirms cancellation is needed

 

3. Approve Order and Send PO

For approved orders:

        Access Helpdesk Mailbox

        Approve the order via the Helpdesk Mailbox

        Once all applicable orders have been approved, update the filter from On Hold to Approved

        Send PO for all approved orders thru Actions > Run Automation
