SOP: Ship Station Stale Orders

 

Check stale orders in SS (Awaiting Shipment and On Hold). Make sure to check all orders 2days old and above, investigate why the order was not yet shipping, or check if it s captured in ZM and POed. 

 

  • Shipstation > Awaiting Shipment/On Hold > Sort by Age > Check Orders 2d old and above. 
  • You can use ZM, Zenship, Spreadsheet and Channel Advisor for further investigation. 
  • Check if needed manual placing of order.
  • Check if it is not captured by ZM hence, the reason for being stale
